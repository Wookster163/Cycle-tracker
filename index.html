How to export from Strava
1
Open Strava.com on your computer (not the app)
2
Go to Settings → My Account → Download or Delete your Account
3
Click Request Your Archive — Strava emails you a link in ~1 hour
4
Download the ZIP, unzip it, and find activities.csv
5
Import that file here — all rides load instantly ✓
How to export from Wahoo ELEMNT

2
Choose Email to send the .fit file to yourself
3
Download the .fit file — FIT import is coming in the next version
